People

Mycelial Communications uses a revolutionary management style that allows our operations to be as responsive as possible. Before accepting each contract, we assemble a team with members that have backgrounds pertinent to the needs of your project and your industry. We ask the right questions and provide the most relevant analysis, guaranteeing that you receive deliverables on time and those deliverables meet or exceed your expectations.

Project Managers

Project Managers work directly with clients to define scope and pricing for the deliverable. Additionally, they are responsible for coordinating the Software Engineers to make sure each solution is exactly to project specifications.

Software Engineers

Software Engineers are code artists; they work directly with the product at its most fundamental levels, working with other Software Engineers to sculpt the hardware and software into a perfect solution.
